The story

I didn't start in tech. I started making beats in Boston.

Jasmati was my producer name — a mashup of my own name and jasmine rice. (Weird, I know. It stuck.) Years of producing taught me how to make complicated systems work together.

In 2022 I found QuickBase and fell in love with workflows. Same skills, different output: patience, systems thinking, making complexity feel manageable.

So I started Jasmati Production. Same name. Workflows instead of beats.

The philosophy

Education over dependency.

Most agencies want you to need them forever. I think that's backwards. I show my work, hand you the docs, and make sure you can run it without me.

If you want me back, great. But you should never have to come back.

Practical over hype.

AI won't change everything overnight. It will save you hours on specific, repetitive tasks if you implement it right. That's it. That's the pitch.
