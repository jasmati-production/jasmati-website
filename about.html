The story

I didn't start in tech. I started making beats in Boston.

Jasmati was my producer name — me plus jasmine rice. Producing taught me to make complex systems work together. In 2022 I found QuickBase, fell for workflows, and started Jasmati Production — same name, workflows instead of beats.

The philosophy

Education over dependency.

Most agencies want you to need them forever. I show my work, hand you the docs, and make sure you can run it without me.

Practical over hype.

AI won't change everything overnight. It will save you hours on repetitive tasks done right. That's the pitch.
